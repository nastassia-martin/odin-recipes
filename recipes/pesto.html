Pesto

Description

Pesto, or Pesto alla Genovese; to refer to the original dish, is a sauce originating in Genoa, the capital city of Liguria, Italy.

It traditionally consists of crushed garlic, European pine nuts, coarse salt, basil leaves, and hard cheese such as Parmigiano-Reggiano or Pecorino Sardo, all blended with olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Parmesan
  • Basil
  • Pine nuts
  • Seasoning, to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a small frying pan over a low heat.
  2. Cook the pine nuts until golden, shaking occasionally.
  3. Put into a food processor with the basil, parmesan, olive oil and garlic cloves.
  4. Whizz until smooth, then season to taste
